Instructions to Read Your Email

Follow the steps below to access and read your emails:

  • 1. Open your email client or webmail service (like Gmail, Outlook, etc.).
  • 2. Log in with your username and password.
  • 3. Once logged in, navigate to your inbox.
  • 4. Click on the email you want to read.
  • 5. Read the contents of the email.
  • 6. Reply, forward, or archive the email as needed.
